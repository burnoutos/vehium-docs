Service history

Every service your workshop has logged against your vehicle, in chronological order.

Where to find it

Open a vehicle from the Vehicles tab, then scroll to the Service history section. The most recent service is at the top.

What each service shows

  • Status: scheduled, in progress, or completed.
  • Date in and date out.
  • Mileage recorded at the start of the job.
  • Inspection items — the checks and replacements performed (e.g. brake pads, oil and filter, timing belt).
  • Parts and labour, with line-item totals.
  • Photos attached by the workshop (before/after, damage, receipts).
  • Notes the workshop has shared with you.
  • Invoice — downloadable PDF for completed services.

Live updates

While a service is open, the status, notes, and photos update in real time. You’ll get a push notification when the workshop:

  • Schedules a service for one of your vehicles.
  • Starts the job (moves to in progress).
  • Completes the job and issues the invoice.

Download the PDF

Completed services have an Invoice button that opens a PDF. The PDF includes:

  • Workshop name, address, VAT number.
  • Your name and the vehicle details.
  • Line items, subtotal, VAT, grand total.
  • A legal reference number that matches the workshop’s bookkeeping.

Tap Share in the PDF viewer to save, email, or print a copy.

Full-vehicle log export

On the vehicle detail page, tap Export service log to generate a single PDF that contains every service, every MOT, and every photo for that vehicle. Useful when selling the car or switching workshop.
