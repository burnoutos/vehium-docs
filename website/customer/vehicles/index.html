Your vehicles

Every car, motorcycle, or truck your workshop has linked to you — in one list.

The vehicles list

Open the Vehicles tab. Each vehicle shows:

  • Make, model, and year.
  • Licence plate.
  • A status badge for MOT expiry: valid, due soon, or expired.
  • The workshop that owns the record (when you’re paired with more than one).

Vehicle detail

Tap a vehicle to open its detail page. You’ll see:

  • Full specs (engine, fuel, colour, VIN if entered).
  • Service history — every service the workshop has logged.
  • MOT history and next expiry date.
  • Photos attached to this vehicle.

Adding vehicles

Vehicles are added by your workshop when they service a car for you. To add a new vehicle:

  1. Ask your workshop to add the vehicle to your client record.
  2. Pull down to refresh in the app.

You can’t add vehicles yourself from the customer app — this keeps your service history authoritative and tied to a real workshop.

Family and shared vehicles

A single vehicle can be linked to several clients — useful for family cars or shared fleet vehicles. If your workshop has linked a car to both you and, say, your partner, it appears in both customer apps and both accounts see the same service history.
