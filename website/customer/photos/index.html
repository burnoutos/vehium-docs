Docs/Customer app/Photos

Photos

Photos of your vehicle — from you and your workshop — all in one gallery.

Where to find them

Open a vehicle, then tap the Photos section. The gallery is sorted newest first and mixes:

  • Workshop photos — before/after, damage, diagnostic shots, receipts.
  • Your photos — anything you’ve added yourself.

Add a photo

  1. Open the vehicle

    From the Vehicles tab, tap the car you want to add a photo to.

  2. Tap + in the Photos section

    Scroll to the Photos section and tap the + button.

  3. Pick a source

    Choose Take photo to use the camera, or Choose from library to pick an existing image.

  4. Save

    Optionally add a short caption, then tap Save.

Uploaded photos sync to your workshop’s copy of the record — useful for reporting damage or showing a noise before you bring the car in.

Service photos

Photos attached to a specific service (e.g. before/after the job) appear both in the vehicle gallery and inside that service in service history.

Storage limits

  • Up to 50 photos per vehicle that you upload yourself.
  • No limit on photos your workshop attaches (they use their own quota).
  • Photos are resized to a maximum of 2048 px on the longest side.

Delete a photo

Open a photo, tap the ··· menu, choose Delete. You can only delete photos you uploaded — workshop photos can only be removed by the workshop.
