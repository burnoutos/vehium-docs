Account

Manage your profile, password, and app preferences.

Profile

In Settings → Profile, you can update:

  • First and last name.
  • Phone number.
  • Profile photo.

Your email is shown for reference but can’t be edited directly — if you need to change it, email info@vehium.com so we can migrate your pairing to the new address.

Change password

  1. Open Password settings

    Go to Settings → Password.

  2. Enter current password

    Type the password you use to sign in today.

  3. Set the new password

    Enter a new password (minimum 8 characters) and tap Save.

If you’ve forgotten your current password, sign out and use Forgot password on the login screen.

Theme and language

  • Theme: light, dark, or follow system.
  • Language: Vehium is available in 8 languages — pick any or follow system.

Sign out

Settings → Sign out ends the session on this device. Your data stays on Vehium; you can sign back in any time on this or any other device.

Export your data

Tap Settings → Export my data to email yourself a ZIP with all your vehicles, service history, and photos in machine-readable form (JSON + originals). Useful for record-keeping or switching workshops.

Delete your account

Settings → Delete account permanently removes your personal data from Vehium. Your service records at the workshop stay with the workshop — they are part of their business records — but they’re no longer linked to you. See the privacy policy for details.

This cannot be undone

Deletion is permanent. Pairing with any workshop is removed and you’ll need to sign up again from scratch if you want to use Vehium later.
