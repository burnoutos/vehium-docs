Enable push notifications

iOS

  1. Open iPhone Settings

    Scroll down to Vehium in the apps list.

  2. Allow notifications

    Tap Notifications, then turn on Allow Notifications.

Android

  1. Open Android Settings

    Go to Settings → Apps → Vehium.

  2. Enable notifications

    Tap Notifications and enable.

Web

The first time you open customer.vehium.com in a browser, you’ll be asked for notification permission. If you missed it, click the lock icon in your address bar and re-enable notifications.

Fine-tune which categories you get

In the app, go to Settings → Notifications. You can switch each category on or off independently:

  • Service updates (scheduled / started / completed).
  • Photos and notes.
  • MOT reminders.
  • Workshop pairing.

Quiet hours

You can set a quiet window (e.g. 10 pm – 7 am) during which push notifications are delivered silently. Emails are unaffected.
