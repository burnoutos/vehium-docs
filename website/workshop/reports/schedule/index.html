Schedule view

A chronological view of what your shop is working on now and booked to work on next.

The three panes

  • Today — every service with today’s date plus any that carried over from yesterday.
  • This week — services scheduled for the next 7 days.
  • Blocked — services stuck in Waiting for parts, oldest first.

What each row shows

  • Scheduled or in-progress time
  • Vehicle (make, model, plate)
  • Owner name
  • Assigned employee
  • Status badge
  • A quick link to the service page

Using the schedule

Click any row to jump to the service. From there you can change status, reassign employees, or update inspection items.

Filtering

  • By assigned employee (multi-select).
  • By status.
Great for morning huddles

Open the Schedule tab on the shop floor TV or a tablet at the start of the day. It shows today’s workload and anything blocked on parts without editing anything.
