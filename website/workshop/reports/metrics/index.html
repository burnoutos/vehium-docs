Reports metrics

Definitions for every number on the Reports page.

MetricDefinition
Total clientsLifetime client count, including deleted clients’ archival records if any.
Recurring clientsClients with 2 or more completed services in the current calendar year.
Total servicesAll services ever created in your workshop.
Services in progressServices whose current status is In progress or Waiting for parts.
Total vehiclesAll vehicle records.
Vehicles in progressVehicles with at least one open service.
Completed vehiclesVehicles whose most recent service is Completed.
Workshop countNumber of workshop locations under this account (enterprise accounts).

Trend comparisons

Each metric card shows a small Δ vs previous period indicator:

  • Green with an up arrow — positive change.
  • Red with a down arrow — negative change.
  • Grey with a flat line — no change.

The comparison period defaults to the previous calendar month, but you can switch to week-over-week or year-over-year using the dropdown at the top.

Data freshness

Reports are served from a cached aggregation. Numbers update within about a minute of any change. If you’ve just completed a service and don’t see it reflected, click the refresh icon to force a re-calculation.
