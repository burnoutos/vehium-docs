Reports

The dashboard is a snapshot; Reports is where you analyse trends and dig into performance.

Who can access

Admins and Managers see the Reports page. Mechanics and Receptionists do not — see Roles & permissions.

The two tabs

Metrics

Totals, comparisons, and trend analytics.

Schedule

What’s scheduled and in-flight across your pipeline.

At a glance

  • Total clients (lifetime).
  • Recurring clients (clients with 2+ services this year).
  • Total services, with in-progress count highlighted.
  • Total vehicles with in-progress vs completed breakdown.
  • Workshop count (for multi-shop accounts — usually 1).
