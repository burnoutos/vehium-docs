Vehicles

Everything Vehium tracks for each car that comes through your workshop — identity, history, MOTs, and photos.

What a vehicle record holds

  • Identity: VIN, license plate, make, model, year, engine type, odometer.
  • Owners: one or more linked clients.
  • Service history: every service order you’ve ever opened for this car.
  • MOT history: dates, results, and expiry.
  • Photos: shots you’ve captured to document condition.
  • Upcoming service: next service date and odometer.
