MOT history

Every MOT test a vehicle has had, with automatic expiry tracking and customer alerts.

Adding an MOT entry

  1. Open the vehicle’s detail page.
  2. Scroll to the MOT history section.
  3. Click + Add MOT.
  4. Fill in the form and save.

MOT fields

FieldRequiredNotes
Test dateYesThe day the test was performed.
Expiry dateYesWhen this certificate expires. Used for alerts.
ResultYesScheduled / Pass / Fail / Advisory.
Testing centerOptionalName of the station.
Certificate numberOptionalOfficial reference.
Advisory notesOptionalFree text — use for advisory or failed-item details.

Expiry tracking

Every entry shows a visual badge based on how far away the expiry date is:

  • Green — more than 60 days away.
  • Amber — 60 to 30 days away.
  • Red — 30 days or fewer, or expired.

Customer alerts

If the vehicle is synced to a Vehium customer-app user (via matching client email), the vehicle owner automatically receives:

  • A push notification 30 days before expiry.
  • A second push 7 days before expiry.
  • An email summary 3 days before expiry.

Editing and deleting entries

Click the row to edit; the pencil icon opens the same form. Delete via the trash icon — deletion is immediate but logged internally.

Tip

Log an Advisory entry with the advisory list in the notes even when the car passes — it’s useful for follow-up reminders and proves due-diligence later.
