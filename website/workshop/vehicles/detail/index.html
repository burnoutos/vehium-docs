Vehicle detail page

The command centre for everything this car has been through.

Info card

At the top of the page, a card summarises the vehicle:

  • Make, model, year
  • License plate & VIN
  • Engine type & current odometer
  • Last service date
  • Next service date & odometer (if set when a service was completed)
  • Edit and Delete actions

Owners

The clients linked to this car. You can add more (the picker is searchable) or unlink any. See Linking vehicles to clients.

Service history

Every service order ever opened for this car:

  • Date, status badge, odometer at service, description, inspection-item count.
  • Expand to preview items and photos.
  • Click to open the service page.
  • Select multiple rows and Print as PDF for a combined log — see Service log & PDF.

MOT history

A log of every MOT test this vehicle has had. See MOT history for the full workflow.

Photos

A gallery of vehicle photos you’ve uploaded or captured. See Vehicle photos.

Visibility

A public/private toggle controls whether every employee can see this vehicle or only admins plus the creator.

Syncs to the customer app

If at least one linked owner has a matching Vehium customer-app account, the vehicle appears in that vehicle owner’s phone app, with MOT alerts and service history visible to them.
