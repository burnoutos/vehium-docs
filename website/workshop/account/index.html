Account

Your personal account settings — different from your workshop profile, which is the business-level information that customers and invoices see.

Where to find it

Click your avatar in the top-right of the app, then choose Settings. The Account tab is the first one in the sidebar.

What’s in Account

Profile & photo

Name, email, phone, avatar.

Change password

Rotate your password any time.

Theme & language

Light/dark mode and 8 UI languages.

Sign out

End your session on this device.

Account vs Workshop settings
