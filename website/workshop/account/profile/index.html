Profile & photo

Your personal details. This is how teammates and the activity log refer to you.

Editable fields

FieldNotes
UsernameAlphanumeric, no spaces. You use this to log in. You can change it — old sessions stay signed in.
First nameShown on invoices when you create them.
Last nameShown alongside your first name in the activity log.
EmailWhere notifications and password resets go.
PhoneMobile number (with country code).
Home numberOptional secondary contact.
AvatarJPEG or PNG, up to 5 MB.

Upload a profile photo

  1. Click the avatar placeholder (the circle with your initials).
  2. Choose Upload from computer or, on mobile, Take a photo.
  3. Crop the square preview.
  4. Hit Save.
Tip

Square photos at 512×512 or larger look sharpest across web, desktop, and mobile.

Saving changes

Click Save at the bottom of the panel. You’ll see a confirmation toast when the update succeeds. If a validation error comes back (e.g. username already taken), the offending field is highlighted in red with an explanatory message.

Who sees what

  • Teammates (other employees) see your name and avatar in service assignments and activity feeds.
  • Customers see only your name on invoices you’ve created — not your photo, email, or phone.
