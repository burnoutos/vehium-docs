Change password

Rotate your password from inside the app whenever you want.

How to change it

  1. Open Settings → Password.
  2. Enter your current password.
  3. Enter a new password. The rules are the same as on sign-up: 12+ characters, uppercase, lowercase, digit, special character.
  4. Type the new password again in Confirm.
  5. Click Update password.
Other sessions stay signed in

Changing your password does not automatically sign out your other devices. If you suspect your account is compromised, change the password and sign out remotely from Sessions.

Forgot your current password?

You can’t change the password without the current one. If you can’t remember it:

  1. Sign out of the app.
  2. On the login screen, click Forgot password.
  3. Enter your account email.
  4. Click the reset link we email you (valid for 15 minutes).
  5. Set a new password.

Security best practices

  • Use a unique password for Vehium — don’t reuse it from other sites.
  • Store it in a password manager.
  • Change it immediately if an employee with your credentials leaves the workshop.
  • Don’t share the account — create a separate employee record for each team member instead.
