Troubleshooting

Solutions to the most common issues. If nothing here fixes it, email info@vehium.com.

Can’t sign in

Forgotten passwords, lost 2FA, locked accounts.

Payment issues

Failed charges, card changes, refunds.

Missing data

Records you expect to see but don’t.

Desktop issues

Install, blank window, auto-update.

Contacting support

Email info@vehium.com from the address on your account. Include:

  • Your workshop name (so we find the right account).
  • The exact error text or a screenshot.
  • The time it happened (with time zone).
  • What device and browser/desktop version.
  • The desktop log if it’s a desktop issue.

Support responds within one business day. Billing issues are prioritized.
