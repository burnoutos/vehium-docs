Release notes

What shipped and when, in reverse chronological order.

v3.1.1

Current version. In-app subscription management and invoice polish.

  • Cancel subscription from inside Vehium — no more Stripe portal trip. Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription schedules the cancellation for the end of your current paid period. Full access stays on until that date, no further charges.
  • Reactivate with one click — while a cancellation is pending, the same screen offers Reactivate subscription to undo it. Billing resumes on its normal cycle. After the period actually ends, just subscribe again as normal. See Cancelling your subscription.
  • Confirmation emails are sent automatically on both cancel and reactivate.
  • Invoice CSV export now uses the dd/MM/yyyy date format for consistency with the on-screen invoice list.
  • Misc bug fixes and polish.

v3.0.6

Maintenance release across all platforms.

  • Improved sign-in reliability — session refresh fixes on slow networks.
  • Phone-number fields now accept all international formats consistently.
  • Faster loading of large vehicle service histories.
  • Misc bug fixes and polish.

v3.0.0

  • Dashboard redesign with month-over-month trend indicators.
  • Service pre-made templates — bundle checklists for reusable application.
  • MOT alerts push automatically to the customer app 30/7/3 days before expiry.
  • Unified push-notification stack (FCM) for iOS and Android.
  • Brand-wide visual refresh.

v2.1

  • Multiple owners per vehicle.
  • Service photos (distinct from vehicle photos).
  • Activity feed on the dashboard.
  • 8-language localization.

v2.0

  • Electron desktop app for Windows and macOS.
  • Stripe subscriptions with 7-day free trial.
  • Role-based permissions for employees.
  • Invoice PDF export with workshop branding.

Get notified about releases

Every major release is announced via in-app banner and to the email address on file. You can also follow vehium.com for product news.
