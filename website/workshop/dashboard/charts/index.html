Revenue & activity charts

How to read the three visual widgets on the dashboard.

30-day revenue line chart

A line chart plotting gross revenue per day for the last 30 days. Hover (or long-press on mobile) a point to see that day’s exact net, VAT, and gross.

  • Missing days (no invoices) render as 0.
  • The accent-coloured area beneath the line gives a sense of volume.
  • Refunds and voids reduce the day’s revenue.

Vehicle brands pie

A pie chart showing the share of your customer base by make (Toyota, Ford, BMW, …). Useful for deciding what tools or spares to stock.

Two switch modes:

  • By count — number of vehicles per brand.
  • By service volume — weights brands by how many services each vehicle has had.

Top clients

A ranked list of clients by total services this year. Click any name to jump to their detail page.

Recent activity feed

The last ten events across every area — new clients, new vehicles, service status changes, invoice creation. Each row links to the underlying record.

Tip

Use the activity feed during a morning huddle — it’s a good way to see what your team did overnight without poking around individual records.
