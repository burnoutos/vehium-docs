Getting started

Everything you need to go from a brand-new account to running your first service in under ten minutes.

What Vehium Workshop is

Vehium Workshop is an all-in-one management platform for auto-repair workshops. One login gives you a dashboard, client records, vehicle records, service orders (with inspection items, photos, and invoices), MOT tracking, staff management, and reports. It runs on web, Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android, and every change syncs instantly.

Who it’s for

  • Garage owners who want end-to-end visibility across customers, cars, and revenue.
  • Service advisors who write up work orders, photograph findings, and invoice customers.
  • Mechanics who inspect vehicles and log results against a checklist.
  • Office staff who schedule appointments and handle billing.

Your first 10 minutes

  1. Sign up — create an account at app.vehium.com or on the mobile app.
  2. Verify your email — click the link we send you.
  3. Set your password — 12+ characters with a mix of letters, digits, and symbols.
  4. First login — your free 7-day trial is already active.
  5. Add your workshop details — name, VAT, address, hours.
  6. Add your first client and their vehicle.
  7. Create your first service and try out an invoice.
Tip

Everything you enter during the trial is kept when you subscribe. There’s no reset.

About the free trial

New accounts get a 7-day trial, no credit card required. Every feature is unlocked during the trial. When it ends, you’ll be prompted to pick a plan before you can create new records. See Your 7-day trial for specifics.
