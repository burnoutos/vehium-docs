Verify your email

We send a verification link to your workshop email address to confirm you own it and to protect the account from being hijacked.

What arrives

Within a minute of signing up, you’ll get an email from no-reply@vehium.com titled something like “Verify your Vehium account”. The email contains a single Verify email button. Click it from the same device if possible.

Security note

The link takes you directly to app.vehium.com/confirm-email (or dev-app.vehium.com on staging). On mobile, the app opens automatically thanks to Apple Universal Links and Android App Links — you don’t need to copy-paste the URL.

Link expiry

The verification link is valid for 15 minutes as a security measure. If you click an expired link, you’ll see a Link expired screen with a button to resend a fresh one.

Email didn’t arrive?

  1. Wait 1–2 minutes. Delivery can be delayed by corporate spam filters.
  2. Check spam / junk. Add no-reply@vehium.com to your contacts or allowlist.
  3. Resend from the confirmation screen, or sign up again with the same address — you won’t create a duplicate account.
  4. Still nothing? Email info@vehium.com from the address you signed up with.

After you verify

Verifying the email activates your account and your 7-day free trial. You’re automatically redirected to the password setup screen.
