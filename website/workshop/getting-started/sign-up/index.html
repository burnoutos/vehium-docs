Sign up

Create a Vehium Workshop account. It takes about a minute, no credit card required.

Where to sign up

You can create your account from any of:

  • Web: app.vehium.com → Create a workshop account
  • Desktop: launch Vehium Workshop on Windows or macOS, then click Create account
  • Mobile: open the Vehium Workshop app from the App Store or Play Store and tap Sign up

What we ask for

FieldRules
First nameRequired
Last nameRequired
UsernameRequired. Letters and numbers only, no spaces. This is what you’ll use to log in.
EmailRequired. Use your workshop email — it’s where the verification link is sent and where customer replies will go.
TermsRequired. You must acknowledge the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
No password yet

Vehium doesn’t ask for your password up front. After sign-up, you’ll receive an email link to set your password on your own device.

After you submit

  1. Account created in pending state

    Your account exists but can’t sign in yet until you verify your email.

  2. Verification email sent

    We email a verification link to the address you provided. Check spam if you don’t see it within a minute or two.

  3. Click the link

    When you click the link, the account activates and your 7-day free trial begins.

  4. Set your password

    You’re dropped into the set-password flow to finish setup.

Common problems

  • Email not arriving: wait 1–2 minutes, then check spam/junk. Add no-reply@vehium.com to your contacts.
  • Username taken: pick a different username. Your workshop name plus a number is a good fallback (e.g. johnsgarage01).
  • Email already registered: go back to the login screen and use forgot password.
