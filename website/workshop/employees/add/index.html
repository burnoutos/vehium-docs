Add an employee

Give a teammate their own login.

How to open the form

  • Employees page → + New employee.
  • Dashboard → New employee quick action.

Fields

FieldRequiredNotes
UsernameYesAlphanumeric, no spaces. Used to log in.
PasswordYesSet an initial password; the employee can change it after first login. Same 12-char rule as your own password.
First nameYesShown on activity feed and invoices.
Last nameYes 
EmailYesWhere password-reset links go.
PhoneOptional 
Home numberOptional 
RolesYesAt least one; multi-select. See Roles & permissions.
Tell them the username + password

Vehium doesn’t automatically email new employees their credentials. Share the username and initial password through a secure channel (in person, signal, 1Password share) and ask them to change the password on first login.

After saving

The employee appears in the Employees list. They can sign in immediately at app.vehium.com or any Vehium app on any device with the username/password you provided. First-login prompts them to change the password.
