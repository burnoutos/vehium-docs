Employees

Each member of your team gets their own login, role, and set of assigned vehicles.

Why separate accounts

  • Activity feed attributes actions correctly (who created this service, who edited that invoice).
  • You can revoke access when someone leaves without disturbing the owner account.
  • Roles limit what each person can see or change.
