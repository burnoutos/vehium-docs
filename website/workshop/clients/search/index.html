Search & filter

Find any client in under a second.

The search box

The top of the Clients list has a single search field. It matches across:

  • First or last name (partial, case-insensitive)
  • Email (partial)
  • Phone number (digits only, ignores spaces and +)

Example queries:

  • mari → Maria Garcia, Marino Rossi, Maristella Conti…
  • @hotmail → every client with a Hotmail address.
  • 07123 → matches any phone containing that sequence.

Keyboard

ShortcutAction
/Focus the search box from anywhere
EscClear and unfocus
EnterOpen the top result
↑ / ↓Navigate results

No results?

  • Try a shorter substring.
  • Check you’re on the right account — clients are scoped per workshop.
  • Confirm the client isn’t under a different spelling. Search ignores accents, so Jose will find José.
Tip

Searching by phone number is the fastest way to pull up the right record when a customer rings in — just type the last four or five digits of their number.
