Client service history

Everything you’ve ever done for this client, across every vehicle they own.

Where to find it

On the client detail page, scroll to Service history. It’s a paged list, newest first.

What each row shows

  • Service date (and completion date if different).
  • Status badge (in progress, waiting for parts, completed, etc.).
  • The vehicle (make, model, plate).
  • Short description.
  • Inspection-item count.
  • A chevron to expand or open the full service page.

Filters

  • Status — narrow by one or multiple.
  • Date range — from / to (inclusive).
  • Vehicle — if the client has several cars.

Expanding a row

Clicking the chevron on a row reveals:

  • The inspection items (categorized).
  • Any photos attached.
  • The invoice total if one was issued.

For the full editing view, click the row itself.

Exporting the history

Vehium doesn’t export client history as a single report today, but you can:

  • Open each service and export its invoice as PDF.
  • Select multiple rows on a vehicle’s page and print them as a combined service log.
