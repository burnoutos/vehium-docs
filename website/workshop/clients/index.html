Clients

A client is a person or company whose vehicles you service. This section shows how to create them, find them fast, and dive into their full history.

What a client record holds

  • Identity — first name, last name.
  • Contact — email, phone (international format supported).
  • Address — street, city, state, postal code, country.
  • Linked vehicles — every car you’ve touched for them.
  • Service history — every service order tied to any of their vehicles.

Email & the customer app

If the client’s email matches a user of the Vehium customer app, their vehicles automatically appear in their vehicle-owner app too — including MOT alerts. Double-check the email field when you create or edit a client.
