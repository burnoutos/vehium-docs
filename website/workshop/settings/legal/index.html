Requesting your data

Under GDPR and similar laws you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Have it exported in a machine-readable format.
  • Have it deleted (subject to legal retention requirements for invoices).

Email info@vehium.com from the address on your account to file a request. We respond within 30 days.

Subprocessors

Vehium uses the following third parties to deliver the service:

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Google Cloud Platform — hosting and storage.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications.
  • Sendgrid — transactional email.

See the privacy policy for the current full list.
