Workshop profile

Your business identity. Fill this in first — it flows onto every invoice and into your public listing.

Fields

FieldRequiredNotes
Company nameYesAs it should appear on invoices.
Company emailYesShown on invoices; used as reply-to for customer messages.
VAT numberOptionalPrinted on invoices where applicable.
PhoneOptionalOn invoices and your public listing.
DescriptionOptionalA one-paragraph blurb for your public listing.
Street addressOptionalPrinted on invoices.
City / state / postal code / countryOptionalDriving directions in the customer app use these.
LogoOptionalPNG, JPEG, or SVG. Square crop recommended (at least 512×512). Max 2 MB.

Units

  • Odometer units — kilometres or miles. Applies everywhere odometer is shown.
  • Currency — detected from your country but overridable.
  • Default VAT rate — pre-fills the VAT% on new invoice lines.
Changes apply everywhere

Edits to the workshop profile update every future invoice instantly. Past invoices are immutable — so if you correct your company name, old invoices keep the old name (which matches most tax authority expectations).

Saving

Click Save at the bottom. A green toast confirms the update.
