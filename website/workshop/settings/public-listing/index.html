Public listing

Opt in to appear as a discoverable workshop in the Vehium customer app.

What it does

With the public listing enabled, vehicle owners browsing the Vehium customer app can find your shop. They see:

  • Your logo and company name.
  • Your description (the paragraph from your profile).
  • Your address and phone.
  • Your working hours.
  • A map pin at your address.

Enabling it

  1. Open Settings → Workshop → Public listing.
  2. Toggle Discoverable to on.
  3. Review the preview that appears on the right.
  4. Click Publish.
Prerequisites

To publish, your workshop profile must include at minimum: company name, email, address, and logo. Vehium will block publishing if any are missing and link you to the fields to fill.

What’s not shown

Customer data, service history, and pricing are never shown to the public. The listing is purely marketing.

Disabling

Toggle Discoverable to off. Your listing disappears from the customer app within about a minute. Existing customers who already have your workshop linked aren’t affected.
