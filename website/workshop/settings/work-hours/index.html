Work hours & breaks

Tell Vehium when you’re open, and when your team is on a break.

Where this shows up

  • The Schedule view greys out hours you’re closed.
  • The public listing (if enabled) shows your open hours to vehicle owners browsing the customer app.
  • Email reminders sent to customers respect your quiet hours.

Setting hours per day

  1. Open Settings → Workshop → Hours.
  2. For each day, toggle Open / Closed.
  3. If open, set From and To times.
  4. Click Save.

Adding breaks

Under each day, click + Add break to insert a closed window (e.g. lunch). You can add several breaks per day. Breaks don’t appear on invoices; they’re just used for scheduling.

Copy Monday to weekdays

Set up Monday the way you like it, then click Copy Monday to Mon–Fri. Saturday and Sunday are set separately; leave either closed if you don’t open that day.

Time zone

All times are interpreted in the time zone set on your workshop profile. If you see wrong hours in reports, check that first.
