Services

A service is any job performed on a vehicle — from a routine oil change to a full overhaul. Services are the heart of Vehium Workshop, tying vehicle, client, inspection findings, photos, and invoice together.

Service lifecycle

  1. Create a service, selecting a vehicle and a date.
  2. Add inspection items — either individually from the catalog, or in bulk from a pre-made template.
  3. Update each item with its condition and notes as work progresses.
  4. Attach photos to document findings.
  5. Add an invoice with line items, prices, and VAT.
  6. Mark complete — set the next service date/odometer.
