Service photos

Photos attached to a specific service order. Perfect for documenting findings, showing customers what you saw, and building a long-term visual record.

Service vs vehicle photos

Two distinct galleries:

  • Service photos live on the service page. They’re specific to that job (e.g. a crack you found on a brake disc during this service).
  • Vehicle photos live on the vehicle page. They’re general, persistent records of the car.

Capturing photos

  1. Open the service.
  2. Scroll to the Photos section.
  3. Click + Add photos.
  4. Pick Take a photo (mobile), Choose from library, or Upload (desktop/web).

Limits

  • JPEG, PNG, HEIC — automatically compressed to max 2 MB on the client before upload.
  • Up to 10 MB raw file size per photo before compression.
  • No hard limit on photos per service.

Viewing and sharing

Click any thumbnail for the full-size viewer (swipe on mobile, arrow keys on web). Service photos are included in the service log PDF when you export multi-service logs.

Customer-facing

Unlike vehicle photos, service photos can be shared with the customer by including them in the printed PDF of the service. A great way to justify charges for non-obvious work.
