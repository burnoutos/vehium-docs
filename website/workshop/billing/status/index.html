Subscription status

Your account is always in one of four states. This is what each means.

The four states

StatusMeaningWhat you can do
Trial active You’re in your free 7-day window. Everything. All features unlocked.
Subscription active A paid subscription is running. Everything. All features unlocked.
Trial ending soon 2 days or less left in trial. Everything — plus a banner reminds you to pick a plan.
Payment required Trial or subscription expired. Sign in and view existing data. Cannot create or edit records until you subscribe.

Refreshing the status

If you’ve just paid but still see Payment required, the Stripe webhook may be a few seconds behind. Click the Refresh button next to the status on Settings → Billing to force a re-sync.

What “payment required” locks

  • Creating or editing clients, vehicles, services, inspection items, employees, invoices.
  • Creating or completing service orders.
  • Uploading photos.
  • Publishing changes to your public listing.

What still works

  • Signing in and signing out.
  • Viewing all existing records.
  • Downloading past invoices as PDF.
  • Access to Settings → Billing so you can subscribe again.
