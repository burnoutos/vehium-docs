Everything about paying for Vehium Workshop — trial, plans, payment methods, and subscription state.

How billing works

Vehium Workshop is a subscription. Every new account starts with a free 7-day trial that unlocks every feature. At the end of the trial, you pick a plan and pay with a credit/debit card through Stripe.

Payments & Stripe

Vehium uses Stripe to process payments. When you pick a plan, you’re redirected to Stripe’s hosted checkout — we never see or store your card number. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified, the strictest compliance level in the industry.

Currencies & tax

Prices are shown in your local currency where supported. VAT is added automatically for eligible regions based on your billing address. All invoices include a VAT breakdown for your records.

Need help?

Billing questions or trouble charging your card? Email info@vehium.com with your account email and we’ll respond within one business day.
