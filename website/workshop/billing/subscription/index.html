Subscription & plans

Compare plans and pick the one that matches your workshop.

Comparing plans

The live plan cards in Settings → Billing show current pricing for your region, including any promotional discounts. The canonical pricing page lives at vehium.com/#pricing.

Pick a plan

  1. Open Settings → Billing.
  2. Review the plan cards (features, monthly price, user seats).
  3. Click Choose plan on the one you want.
  4. You’re redirected to Stripe checkout in a new tab.
  5. Enter your card, confirm your billing address, and submit.
  6. Stripe redirects you back to Vehium. The page shows Subscription active.
Secure checkout

Card details are entered and stored on Stripe’s side. Vehium only receives a token that represents the subscription — we never see the card number.

Upgrading and downgrading

To switch plans:

  1. Open Settings → Billing.
  2. Click Change plan next to your current subscription.
  3. Pick the new plan.
  4. Confirm the proration on the summary screen — upgrading charges the difference immediately; downgrading credits the difference to your next invoice.

Cancelling your subscription

Subscriptions are managed by Stripe. To stop a subscription you have to cancel it inside the Stripe customer portal — this is the only place a cancellation can be made.

  1. Open Settings → Billing.
  2. Click Manage subscription. This opens the Stripe customer portal in a new tab.
  3. Click Cancel plan and confirm.
  4. Your subscription stays active through the paid-through date, then stops automatically.

After the period ends the account moves to Payment required — you can still sign in and view existing data, but creating or editing records is locked. See Subscription status. Data retention follows the rules in the trial article.

Stripe will keep charging until you cancel

If you stop using Vehium but never cancel in the Stripe portal, Stripe will continue charging your card every month for as long as the card stays valid. Signing out, uninstalling the desktop or mobile app, or closing your browser does not cancel the subscription — only the Stripe customer portal does.

Deleting your workshop cancels the subscription for you

If you delete your workshop instead, the Stripe subscription is cancelled automatically — at the end of the current billing period during the 30-day grace, or immediately if you choose instant deletion. You don’t need to cancel in the Stripe portal separately.

Invoices & receipts

After every successful charge Stripe emails you a receipt. You can also download a PDF invoice for any past charge from Settings → Billing → Invoices.
