Your 7-day free trial

Every new Vehium Workshop account begins with a 7-day free trial. Here’s what that means.

What’s included

Every feature is fully unlocked during the trial:

  • Unlimited clients, vehicles, services, and employees
  • Inspection items, pre-made service templates, photos
  • Invoicing with PDF export
  • Dashboard, reports, MOT tracking
  • Web + desktop + mobile apps with full sync

When it starts

The clock starts the moment you verify your email — not when you sign up. So if you create an account but take a few days to click the verification link, you still get a full 7 days of trial from activation.

No credit card required

You won’t be asked for a payment method to start the trial. Vehium only asks for a card when you actively choose a plan.

Trial ending soon

With 2 days or less remaining, a banner appears across the app reminding you to pick a plan. Your data isn’t touched — the banner is just a nudge.

When it ends without a plan

If you don’t subscribe before the trial ends, your account moves into the Payment required state:

  • You can still sign in.
  • You can still view every record you’ve created.
  • You cannot create or edit new records until you subscribe.
  • Your data is preserved — subscribing at any point restores full access instantly.
Data retention

If your account sits in Payment required for 30 days or more without subscribing, Vehium moves the account to cold storage. Records aren’t deleted immediately, but exports may be temporarily unavailable. Subscribe within 90 days to restore everything.

How to convert before the trial ends

  1. Open Settings → Billing.
  2. Review the plan cards and click Choose plan.
  3. You’re redirected to Stripe checkout in a new tab.
  4. Enter your card, confirm, and return to the app — subscription activates within a few seconds.
